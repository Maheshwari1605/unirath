Revenue Cycle Management (RCM)

Complete Solution for Healthcare Providers

Overview

Our comprehensive Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) services streamline the entire revenue cycle from patient registration to final payment collection. We ensure maximum revenue capture while maintaining strict compliance with healthcare regulations including HIPAA, ensuring your practice operates efficiently and profitably.

With over 99% claim accuracy and reduced AR days, we help healthcare providers, billing firms, and hospitals optimize their financial performance while focusing on patient care.

Our RCM Process Flow

1

Patient Registration

Comprehensive demographic data collection, insurance verification, and eligibility checks. We ensure all patient information is accurate and up-to-date before any services are rendered.

  • Demographic data entry and validation
  • Insurance verification and eligibility checks
  • Prior authorization management
  • Patient financial responsibility estimation
↓
2

Medical Coding

Expert medical coding using ICD-10, CPT, and HCPCS codes with 99% accuracy rate. Our certified coders ensure proper documentation and coding compliance.

  • ICD-10 diagnosis coding
  • CPT procedure coding
  • HCPCS level II coding
  • Modifier application and validation
↓
3

Charge Entry & Claim Submission

Automated claim scrubbing and submission to payers. We ensure all claims are clean, accurate, and submitted in a timely manner to maximize reimbursement.

  • Charge capture and entry
  • Automated claim scrubbing
  • EDI submission to payers
  • Real-time claim status tracking
↓
4

Payment Posting

Electronic remittance processing and reconciliation. We accurately post all payments, adjustments, and denials to maintain accurate accounts receivable.

  • ERA processing and posting
  • Manual payment posting
  • Adjustment and write-off management
  • Payment reconciliation
↓
5

AR Follow-up & Denial Management

Proactive follow-up, denial analysis, and appeals. We work diligently to resolve outstanding claims and minimize write-offs.

  • Automated AR follow-up workflows
  • Denial analysis and root cause identification
  • Appeal preparation and submission
  • Payment plan management
↓
6

Reporting & Analytics

Real-time dashboards and performance metrics. We provide comprehensive reporting to help you make informed business decisions.

  • Real-time performance dashboards
  • Key performance indicators (KPIs)
  • Financial reports and analysis
  • Trend analysis and forecasting
